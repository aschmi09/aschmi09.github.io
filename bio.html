Aviva Schmitz

Oakland, California native Aviva Schmitz is a content marketing intern at legal technology startup UpCounsel. She currently studies at at Tufts University in Medford, Massachusetts and expects to earn degrees in English and Spanish upon her graduation in May of 2018. She has served also as an editor and contributing writer for publications such as The Culture Trip, the Tufts Daily, and satirical magazine The Zamboni.
